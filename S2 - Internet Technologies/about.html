WHO EVEN ARE WE

We are a bunch of friends that thoroughly enjoy going to hikes and getting others to hike with us. We have been going to hikes ever since we were children of the ages of 6-7 and we haven’t stopped ever since.
While we mostly hike for fun, we recently have found that there actually is a lot of research done on the topic of what kind of health benefits hiking provides in the short and long term.
Not only improved physical but also mental health comes as a result of some good hiking sessions.
So what are you waiting for? Pack your equipment and shoot us an email, we are waiting for you. Let’s have some fun and let's live better lives.
